# Research Brief

## Project

Xenium-oriented synovial tissue microarray design for an 80-patient cohort.

## Objective

Design and compare TMA layouts that fit within the Xenium usable chamber while preserving patient-level replication and robust orientation. The current default design splits 80 patients into 2 TMAs with 40 patients per TMA.

## Study Type

Descriptive design optimization and method validation. This is not a causal, prognostic, or diagnostic performance analysis.

## Primary Design Metrics

- Patient core positions per TMA.
- Mean and minimum balanced cores per patient.
- Total patient core surface area.
- Total core surface area divided by total Xenium chamber area.
- Orientation marker cost.
- Asymmetry score.

## Current Working Recommendation

Use 2 TMAs, 0.6 mm cores, hexagonal disposition, 1.0 mm minimum edge-to-edge spacing, and 4 compact asymmetric empty orientation holes per TMA.

This gives 108 patient cores per TMA, 216 patient cores across 2 TMAs, and an average of 2.70 cores per patient.
